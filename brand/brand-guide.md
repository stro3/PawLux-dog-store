# PawLux Brand Guide

## Brand Overview

**Name**: PawLux
**Tagline**: "Elevated Essentials for Your Best Friend"
**Niche**: Pet > Dogs — premium accessories and wellness products
**Mission**: Provide dog owners with thoughtfully designed, high-quality products that enhance the lives of their pets.

## Target Customer

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 25–45 |
| Income | Mid-to-high |
| Values | Quality, aesthetics, pet wellness |
| Behavior | Researches before buying, reads reviews, willing to pay more for premium |
| Pain Points | Cheap products that break, harmful ingredients, ugly designs |

## Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Premium, warm, trustworthy
- **Language**: Confident but approachable, never condescending
- **Avoid**: Overly casual slang, aggressive sales language, clickbait

Examples:
- YES: "Crafted with veterinarian-approved materials for lasting comfort."
- NO: "OMG your dog will LOVE this!! Buy now before it's gone!!!"

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary | Deep Forest Green | `#1B4332` | Headers, buttons, primary CTA |
| Secondary | Warm Cream | `#F5E6CC` | Backgrounds, card surfaces |
| Accent | Golden Tan | `#D4A574` | Highlights, badges, hover states |
| Text Dark | Charcoal | `#2D2D2D` | Body text |
| Text Light | Soft Gray | `#6B7280` | Secondary text, captions |
| White | Pure White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, card surfaces |

### Color Ratio (60/30/10 Rule)

| Ratio | Color | Applied To |
|---|---|---|
| 60% Primary (#1B4332) | Deep Forest Green | Announcement bar, header text, footer background, all buttons, CTA backgrounds, heading text, product titles, sticky ATC bar |
| 30% Secondary (#F5E6CC) | Warm Cream | Newsletter section background, USP section background, image-with-text backgrounds, card hover backgrounds, popup discount code box |
| 10% Accent (#D4A574) | Golden Tan | Sale badges, hover states, input focus borders, footer link hover, trust badge icons, product labels, star ratings |

## Logo Usage

- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the paw icon on all sides
- Minimum size: 32px height for digital
- Always use on white, cream, or dark green backgrounds
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo

## Photography Style

- Natural lighting, warm tones
- Dogs in real environments (home, outdoors)
- Clean backgrounds, minimal clutter
- Show product in use whenever possible
