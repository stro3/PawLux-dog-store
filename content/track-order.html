Enter your order number and email address to check your shipping status. You can find your order number in the confirmation email we sent when you placed your order.

Need Help?

Where is my tracking number?

Once your order ships, we send a tracking number to your email. Check your inbox and spam folder for an email from PawLux with the subject "Your order has shipped."

My tracking shows no updates

Tracking information can take 24–48 hours to update after your order ships. If there are no updates after 5 business days, contact us at support@pawlux.com.

Estimated delivery times

Standard shipping: 7–14 business days. Express shipping: 3–5 business days. Processing time (1–4 business days) is separate from shipping time.
