Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 1–4 business days depending on the product. Delivery takes 7–14 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes. All orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping within the United States.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking link. You can also visit our Track Order page.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within the United States. International shipping is coming soon.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all unused items in original packaging. Contact us at support@pawlux.com with your order number to start a return.

Can I exchange a product for a different size?

Yes. Email us within 30 days of delivery and we will arrange the exchange. Return shipping for exchanges is free.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will send a replacement at no cost.

Products

How do I choose the right size?

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up. You can also email us your dog's breed, weight, and measurements for a personal recommendation.

Are your products safe for puppies?

Most products are suitable for dogs of all ages. The calming treats are recommended for dogs 12 weeks and older. Check each product description for specific age guidance.

Are the calming treats safe for daily use?

Yes. Our calming treats are veterinarian-formulated with natural ingredients and are safe for daily use. Follow the dosage guidelines on the packaging based on your dog's weight.

Account & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All transactions are processed through Shopify's PCI-compliant, SSL-encrypted checkout. We never store your card details.

Email us at support@pawlux.com and we will get back to you within 24 hours.
