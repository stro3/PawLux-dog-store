Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–4 business days (Monday through Friday, excluding holidays). You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Delivery Time

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping 7–14 business days $5.99 (Free on orders over $75)
Express Shipping 3–5 business days $14.99

Shipping Coverage

We currently ship within the United States. International shipping is not yet available but is planned for the near future.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, a tracking number will be sent to your email. You can also check your order status on our Track Order page.

Returns

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied. If a product does not meet your expectations, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Calming treats and other consumable products cannot be returned once opened
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective

How to Start a Return

  1. Email support@pawlux.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. Our team will respond within 24 hours with a return authorization and shipping instructions
  3. Ship the item back using the provided instructions
  4. Refund will be processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? Contact us and we will arrange a free exchange. Once we receive your original item, the replacement will ship within 2 business days.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us immediately with photos. We will ship a replacement at no cost — no return needed.
