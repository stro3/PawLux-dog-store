About PawLux

Elevated Essentials for Your Best Friend

Our Story

PawLux started with a simple frustration: finding quality dog products that actually last. We were tired of harnesses that frayed after a month, beds that went flat in weeks, and treats loaded with ingredients we could not pronounce. So we built something better.

Every product in our collection goes through a rigorous selection process. We work with licensed veterinarians to evaluate materials and formulations. We test durability in real conditions with real dogs. And we stand behind everything with our 30-day money-back guarantee because we believe you should never have to settle for less when it comes to your dog.

What We Stand For

Quality First

We reject over 90% of products we evaluate. Only the best make it to our store. Non-toxic, durable, and built to last — no exceptions.

Vet Approved

Every wellness product is reviewed by licensed veterinarians. We never sell anything we would not give our own dogs.

Honest Pricing

Premium does not mean overpriced. We keep margins fair and pass savings to you through free shipping on orders over $75.

Real Support

Questions about sizing, materials, or your order? Our team responds within 24 hours. Every time.

Our Promise

If any PawLux product does not meet your expectations, contact us within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. No hoops. No fine print. Just a straightforward guarantee that your satisfaction matters.
